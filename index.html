It's not just a coffee It's Starbucks

Starbucks Corporation is an American coffeehouse chain. Founded in Seattle in 1971 by Jerry Baldwing, Zev Siegl and Gordon Bowker, the company adopted the name "Starbucks" in 1987 after its acquisition by Howard Schultz. Starbucks locations sell their own brand of coffee (ground or whole bean), tea, beverages, pastries, utensils and coffee machines.
